✦ what is auditproof?

auditproof is a free & open source roblox script for Bad Business with Phantom Forces support coming rlly soon!!

no key system. no discord requirement. no paywalls. just paste and play xoxo ♥ the script is fully open source so u can literally see every line of code on our github. we are NOT sketchy we promise lmaooo
